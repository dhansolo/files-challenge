Ash Ketchum

Ash Ketchum is a Pokemon trainer from Pallet Town. Through his journeys, he has made many friends and defeated many gym leaders, all with the help of his fellow sidekick, Pikachu. But he has also made enemies, including Team Rocket and his rival, Gary. Unlike some trainers, Ash is very skilled in training his Pokemon and also respects and loves them. Although he has been 10 years old for some time now, his life has been much more eventful than most of our lives.

Ash appears in the popular anime/cartoon, Pokemon. The series follows Ash's journey to become the greatest Pokemon trainer ever. He is followed by his friends, Brock and Misty, and his fellow Pokemon, including Pikachu. Ash and his friends encounter many different Pokemon through their journeys, including some very rare ones. Many challenges arise for Ash and him and his friends always find a way to overcome those obstacles all while learning about what it means to be a trainer and what it takes to be the best.
